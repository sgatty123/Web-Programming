RULES


1.The player has to choose a Chocolate of his choice from a given selection.

2.He then places some amount at stake.

3.Three dice are then rolled by clicking on the button 'Roll'.

4.If the chocolate that is selected by the player shows up at least on one single dice,

then the player wins, if it doesn’t he loses the amount.

5.If the chocolate chosen shows up on more than one dice, the player’s winning amount multiplies.
